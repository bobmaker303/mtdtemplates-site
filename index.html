Frequently Asked Questions

Is this template actually MTD compliant?

This template is designed for MTD-compliant record keeping with categories aligned to HMRC's SA105 form. To submit to HMRC, you'll pair it with bridging software like AbraTax (from ~£36/year), which connects your spreadsheet data to HMRC's systems.

Does it work in Excel AND Google Sheets?

Yes. The template is built in Excel (.xlsx) format and is fully compatible with Google Sheets. All formulas, dropdowns, and formatting work in both.

How many properties can I track?

Up to 10 properties in a single spreadsheet. Each transaction is tagged to a specific property, and summaries aggregate across your entire portfolio.

What about Section 24 mortgage interest?

Fully handled. Enter your annual mortgage interest for each property, and the template automatically calculates your 20% tax credit — separate from your property expenses, exactly as HMRC requires.

Do I still need an accountant?

This template handles your record keeping and quarterly summaries. We always recommend consulting a qualified accountant for your specific tax situation, especially for your Final Declaration due each January.

What if I'm not affected by MTD yet?

Even if you're below the threshold, proper digital records will save you time at tax return season. And when your threshold arrives (£30k in 2027, £20k in 2028), you'll already be prepared.

Due to the digital nature of the product, we cannot offer refunds once downloaded. However, if you experience any issues, contact us and we'll do our best to help.
